Our Mission

Christian Youth in Action (CYA) is the youth arm of the Christian Education Department under the African Inland Church (AIC). Our mission is to:

  • Provide youth with meaningful Bible studies that strengthen their faith
  • Train young people to be responsible and godly individuals
  • Prepare youth for service opportunities within the church
  • Build a community of young believers who support and encourage one another
  • Develop future leaders for the African Inland Church
